All Holy Homies

About Us

All Holy Homies was created out of a desire to better know the holy men and women who loved Christ with their whole selves. The name comes from the Litany of Saints, when Christians ask, “All holy men and women, pray for us!” This, combined with the idea that the saints can be our close friends, homies even, helped give All Holy Homies its name. We strive to create fun necklaces with beachy vibes that incorporates the well-researched colors associated with the life of each saint they bear. Each necklace is handcrafted individually as a true labor of love, with you and your prayer intentions in mind! In addition to our standard necklaces, we can create custom designs based around your favorite or patron saints, favorite colors, different sizes, or medals that you already have that mean a lot to you. Please check our FAQs for more information.

FAQs

General

How do I purchase a necklace?

Right now, all you have to do is reach out to us via email or DM. Let us know which necklace, what size, and where we should ship it. Feel free to include your prayer intentions. Once we receive your order and payment, we will make your necklace, process the order, and ship it. We will be in touch with you about when your order gets sent and what your tracking number is.

How do I pay?

We accept payments via Venmo, Cash App, or a good old check/cash if in person! If you are placing a custom order, please wait to send payment until we confirm the details of your necklace.

Custom Orders

(Please note that custom orders will have a higher cost than our standard offerings.)

I own a specific medal that I want used on my necklace. How does that work?

Using a medal with sentimental meaning is so special! We would ask you to send the medal to us, and then we will get to work creating your unique piece for you. Everything else about the process will be normal.

I want a different length necklace than is offered.

Just let us know in inches what size you’d like it to be and we will fulfill your request!

Can I ask for a necklace to be made with different colors?

Absolutely. We will work with you to find colors that match your vision as best as possible.

My patron saint isn’t offered!

Whether it’s a design we haven’t made yet, your saint of choice is obscure and it’s hard to find a medal for them, or you are one of our Orthodox brothers and sisters, we will work with you to find a medal for your saint. Depending on how difficult they are to find, we may need to order a custom engraved medal. If this is the case we will contact you with a full cost disclosure before we place a custom engraving order.
